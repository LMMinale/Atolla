About

Mesophotic
Reef
Research

The Atolla Research Initiative documents and protects mesophotic reef ecosystems — the poorly understood zone between 30 and 181m. Led by Dr Sarah Rowley, the programme combines over 40 years of CCR diving expertise with cutting-edge phylogenomics to discover new species and understand the ecology of deep reefs before they are lost.
