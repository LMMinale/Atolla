This Privacy Policy explains how Atolla Research Initiative, operated by SJR LLC ("Atolla", "we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you visit atolla.bio or interact with us.

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate this site. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. This policy is written in plain English — if something is unclear, contact us.

Our baseline: If you only browse this website and do not submit any form, we do not collect any personally identifiable information about you.
01

Who We Are

Atolla Research Initiative is a mesophotic reef science and data programme operated by SJR LLC, led by Dr Sarah Rowley. Our research focuses on coral ecosystems at depths of 30–181 metres across the Indo-Pacific.

For the purposes of data protection law, the data controller is:

SJR LLC / Atolla Research Initiative

Email: privacy@atolla.bio

Website: atolla.bio

02

What We Collect

We currently collect personal data in two ways:

Newsletter sign-up

When you subscribe to our newsletter via the sign-up form on the site, we collect:

  • Your email address

We do not require your name to subscribe. Providing your email is entirely voluntary.

Contact form

When you send us a message via the contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message
  • Any additional information you choose to include

Technical data (automatic)

Like most websites, our hosting infrastructure may automatically log basic technical data when you visit, including your approximate IP address, browser type, and the pages you view. This data is used solely for server security and uptime monitoring. It is not linked to any personal profile.

Coming soon: We plan to add website analytics (to understand how visitors use the site) and may introduce functional cookies. When we do, this policy will be updated and you will be given the opportunity to consent. Until then, we do not use analytics or tracking cookies.
Data type When collected Required?

Name, email, message Contact form submission Yes (to reply to you)

03

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the purpose for which you provided it:

  • Email address (newsletter): To send you updates about Atolla's research, expeditions, and publications. Each email includes a one-click unsubscribe link.
  • Contact form data: To respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list based on a contact form submission unless you explicitly request it.
  • Server logs: To maintain site security and diagnose technical issues. Not used for any profiling or marketing purpose.

We will never use your data to send unsolicited commercial messages, share it with advertisers, or build profiles for targeting purposes.

Legal basis (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Consent — for newsletter subscriptions. You can withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing.
  • Legitimate interests — for contact form responses (replying to someone who contacts us) and server security logging.
04

Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

Newsletter email addresses are stored with our email service provider. Contact form submissions are received by email and stored in our operational email inbox. Neither is shared with third parties beyond what is described in Section 5 below.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we do our best to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use third-party tools to operate the site and send newsletters (for example, an email service provider). These providers are contractually restricted from using your data for any purpose other than providing their service to us.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Atolla, its team, or the public.
  • Business transfer: If Atolla Research Initiative or SJR LLC is transferred or restructured, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified in advance if this occurs.

In all other cases, we ask for your explicit consent before sharing.

06

How Long We Keep Your Data

Data type Retention period

07

Cookies

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website.

What we currently use

At present, atolla.bio uses only the basic session cookies necessary for the site to function correctly — for example, to remember your session if you submit a form. These cookies do not track you across other sites and are deleted when you close your browser.

We do not currently use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party tracking cookies.

Planned update: We intend to add website analytics in the near future. When we do, we will implement a cookie consent banner and update this section to describe exactly what is being set and why. You will always be able to opt out.

How to control cookies

You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse certain cookies, some parts of this site may become inaccessible or not function properly. For more information about cookies, visit allaboutcookies.org.

08

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you have certain rights over your personal data. We honour all of the following:

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

Deletion

Ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").

Objection

Object to us processing your data for a specific purpose.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Withdraw consent

Unsubscribe or revoke consent at any time, no questions asked.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@atolla.bio. We will respond within 30 days. We will never charge a fee for a legitimate request.

If you are in the EEA or UK and believe we have not handled your data lawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

California residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your personal information, and opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your California rights, contact us at privacy@atolla.bio.

09

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10

Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy when our data practices change — in particular when we add analytics or cookie tracking. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

If you are subscribed to our newsletter, we will notify you of significant changes by email before they take effect.
